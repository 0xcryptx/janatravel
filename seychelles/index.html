Welcome to Seychelles

Discover white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, tropical forests, and some of the most beautiful island scenery in the Indian Ocean. Seychelles is perfect for romantic escapes, family holidays, island hopping, nature trips, and relaxing luxury stays.

Best Time to Visit

Seychelles is warm all year round, with average temperatures around 24°C to 30°C. The drier months are usually from May to September, while December to February can bring more rain. For calmer seas and beach days, April, May, October, and November are often great months to visit.

Culture & Island Life

Seychelles has a relaxed island culture shaped by African, European, Indian, and Creole influences. Visitors can enjoy colorful markets, local food, nature reserves, peaceful beaches, and warm island hospitality.

From Mahé and Praslin to La Digue and remote islands, each area offers a different experience - from lively local life to private luxury escapes.

Popular Areas in Seychelles

Victoria

Located on Mahé island, Victoria is the capital of Seychelles and a great place to experience local markets, shops, colonial architecture, and island culture before heading to the beaches or nearby resorts.

Praslin

Praslin is known for beautiful beaches, peaceful resorts, and the famous Vallée de Mai Nature Reserve. It is a great choice for travelers who want nature, relaxation, and easy access to nearby islands.

La Digue

La Digue is one of Seychelles’ most iconic islands, loved for its calm atmosphere, bicycle-friendly roads, granite rock beaches, and the famous Anse Source d’Argent. It is perfect for couples, photography, and relaxed island days.

Aldabra

Aldabra is one of Seychelles’ most important natural wonders and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is famous for its giant tortoises and untouched ecosystem, making it a symbol of the country’s rich biodiversity.
