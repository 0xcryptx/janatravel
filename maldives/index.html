Welcome to Maldives

Escape to turquoise lagoons, white-sand beaches, luxury island resorts, and unforgettable ocean adventures. The Maldives is perfect for honeymoons, family holidays, romantic escapes, diving trips, and peaceful private-island stays.

Best Time to Visit

The Maldives is beautiful all year round, but the most popular months are usually December to April, when the weather is sunnier and the sea is calmer. From May to November, prices may be lower, with a higher chance of rain and tropical showers.

Culture & Island Life

Maldives is more than just beaches. Visitors can enjoy local island culture, fresh seafood, traditional music, water sports, and peaceful resort life. From the capital Malé to private island resorts in Ari Atoll, Baa Atoll, and North Malé Atoll, every area offers a different kind of experience.

Popular Areas in Maldives

Malé

The capital city and main arrival point, perfect for short cultural stops, local markets, mosques, and quick city tours before heading to the islands.

North & South Malé Atolls

Great for first-time visitors because they are close to the airport and have many luxury resorts, beaches, water sports, and easy speedboat transfers.

Ari Atoll

Known for stunning resorts, diving spots, whale shark experiences, and beautiful overwater villas. A great choice for couples, honeymooners, and ocean lovers.

Baa Atoll

A peaceful luxury area famous for marine life, nature, and premium island resorts. Ideal for travelers looking for privacy, relaxation, and high-end stays.
